We are passionate about pizza!

Our mission

We want to share the best pizza on this planet with you. By selecting the best ingredients and with help of the best chefs in the world we are providing the best experience straight from Itlay that could be experienced in every part of the globe!

History

The world of pizza project started in the summer of 2007 and was a result of cooperation of the best pizza specialists at that time. We wanted to allow the people to try the most authentic italian recipes. We started a number of restaurants in 89 countries and we are still growing.

Our Recipes

We travelled across Italy and asked local residents about their secret recipes that make their dough so special. After some research done by our experts we came up with the most revolutionary ideas and our recipes are considered the best in the world.
